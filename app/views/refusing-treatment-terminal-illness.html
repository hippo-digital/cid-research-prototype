Refusing treatment if I have a terminal illness

A terminal illness is an illness that has reached a stage where it cannot be cured and is likely to cause a person to die within a limited amount of time.

There are many conditions that can have a terminal stage, including cancer, heart failure, renal failure and lung disease.
