Decide when to refuse treatment for Other scenarios

The four scenarios included in this form cover the main conditions that may lead you to lose mental capacity and require a life-sustaining treatment.

!Warning

You do not have to write any additional refusals of treatment here if you do not want to.

If you do choose to write something here your refusal of treatment needs to be clear to the doctor reading it. This means you need to include:

  • the treatment you want to refuse
  • the scenario you want to refuse it in

For example, some people want to include "I refuse CPR in every situation, regardless of the cause or prognosis".
