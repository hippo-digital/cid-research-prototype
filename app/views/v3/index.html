Make an advance decision (living will)

You can use this free online service to make an advance decision (living will). You can also use it to make an advance statement.

You can use this service if you:

  • are 18 or older
  • have mental capacity
  • live in the UK

This service will take you through the form step-by-step. We'll use your answers to create your form that you can save, print, sign and get witnessed.

You then need to share a copy with your GP and those close to you.

Important:

Advance decisions are legally binding in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland. They are not legally binding in Scotland but you can still use this service to create an advance decision as they should be taken into account by doctors.
