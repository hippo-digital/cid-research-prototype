Do you know your CHI number? (optional)

Your CHI number is a 10 digit number, for example 485 777 3456. You can find it on any letter the NHS has sent you, on a prescription, or by logging in to a GP practice online service.

You do not need to include your Community Health Index (CHI) number in your advance decision, but it could be useful for doctors to help locate your medical record.
